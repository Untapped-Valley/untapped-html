Our Team

Meet the people behind Untapped Valley!

Collin Wang

Chief Executive Officer

Collin is currently a Milliken Mills H.S. in Markham, Ontario in the IB enrichment Program. He will be serving as the Chief Executive Officer at Untapped Valley and is excited to lead the executive team. After attending the Wharton Global Youth Meet Up, Collin found a personal interest within the business department, specifically sports business. With Untapped Valley, he has a high ambition of bringing the organization into the market of entrepreneurs and wishes to open a business-tech related competition. He is thrilled to be working with other individuals that share the same passion as he does for business.

Marcus Lee

Chief Operating Officer

Marcus is currently a senior at Milliken Mills H.S. in Markham, Ontario in the IB enrichment program. He will be serving as the Chief Operations Officer at the Untapped Valley and looks forward to the leadership opportunity. His main interests lie within the intersections of business-tech as a result of his admission to UC Berkeley’s CS Pre-collegiate, as well as his experiences in business competitions. With Untapped Valley, he hopes to further the outreach into the market of entrepreneurial minds as seen in iconic Silicon Valley; and encourage greater involvement in the field of business-tech. Overall, he is eager to work alongside ambitious individuals like himself to make this year a huge success.

Bob Yan

Chief Marketing Officer

Bob is currently a senior at the Milliken Mills High School in Markham, Ontario in the IB Diploma Programme. He will be serving as the Untapped Valley's Chief Marketing Officer. He looks forward to aid the development of the business plans, outreach, and global collaborations for the coming future. He will use his diverse business and management experience to present a holistic competition experience for students. Through Untapped Valley he hopes to provide exceptional business-tech opportunities for students locally and globally.

Cindy Ma

Chief Marketing Officer

Cindy is currently a senior at The Country Day School in King City, Ontario. She will be serving as one of Untapped Valley’s Chief Marketing Officers and is excited to work alongside her fellow executives to organize workshops and build partnerships. She hopes to further explore business relations through the organization and aid in providing educational experiences for everyone. Overall, Cindy looks forward to furthering the future of Untapped Valley!

Andrew Qiao

Chief Technology Officer

Andrew is currently a senior student at Pierre Elliott Trudeau High School in Markham, Ontario. This year, he will be serving as Chief Technology Officer and is extremely excited to use his technological skills to organize events and activities for Untapped Valley. Through Untapped Valley, he is thrilled to be able to utilize his technological capabilities to aid the community around him. His passions lie in many areas, including technology and leadership. In addition, he is also an avid tennis player and enjoys coaching the sport as well. His goal with the organization is to expand opportunities towards as many students as possible, and guiding them towards reaching their objectives! Andrew is always excited to meet new people, so feel free to reach out to him.
